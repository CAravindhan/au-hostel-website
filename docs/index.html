Establishment of CEG Hostels

The CEG Hostels are established for the welfare of the students and are under the direct control of the Institution. CEG Hostels comprises of 20 blocks (located at CEG Campus), out of which boys are accommodated in 12 Blocks and Girls in 8 Blocks.
